Why it's nice

Small tool, exactly one job — done well.

No dashboards, no settings to wrangle. It sits in your tray and waits for you to select a word.

Works in every app

Browsers, PDF readers, Word, chat — anywhere you can select text.

Meaning, not pronunciation

Just the definition and part of speech. No phonetics or audio clutter.

Your clipboard is safe

Whatever you copied stays put. Lookups never overwrite it.

Nothing is stored

No logging, no history, no disk writes. Lookups live in memory only.

Feather-light

A tiny tray app. Start it once and forget it's running.

Free forever

Powered by the open dictionaryapi.dev. No key, no subscription.
