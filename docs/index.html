What it actually does

It sits in your tray and waits for you to select a word.

everywhereadverb

In any app where you can select text: browsers, PDF readers, Word, chat windows, comment boxes.

definitionnoun

The meaning and the part of speech. Phonetics and audio are deliberately left out.

clipboardnoun

Yours, untouched. Whatever you copied is restored after every lookup.

nothingpronoun

What gets written to disk. Lookups live in memory for the session, then vanish.

lightweightadjective

A small tray program. Start it once and forget that it is running.

freeadjective

No key and no subscription. Definitions come from the open dictionaryapi.dev.

The whole interaction

From an unfamiliar word to its meaning, without losing your place.

1

Select a word

Double-click it, or drag to highlight it, in any app.

2

Read the definition

A popup appears at your cursor within a moment.

3

Carry on

Click away, switch windows, or simply wait. It fades on its own.
